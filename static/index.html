I am intrigued by the problems Munchee is solving in the food industry. Munchee will eliminate the bias in current food reviews introduced by centralized systems and will allow users to monetize from their food reviews.

CURRENT PROBLEMS

  • With current CENTRALIZED platforms operated by Yelp, FourSquare, etc., there is a lack of trust and transparency among users and restaurants as issues like review manipulation, fake reviews, spam and low quality reviews exist.

  • Users and content creators (foodies, food bloggers and influencers) have a hard time monetizing on their food passion and content contribution due to the lack of incentive for the end-users; also, those who have positive experience are less likely to write reviews than those who have negative experience.

  • Restaurant owners are charged exorbitant amounts in terms of interest rates, fees and surcharge for their services by existing CENTRALIZED financial networks such as VISA, Mastercard, AmericanExpress.

TOKEN MODEL

The Munchee token (MUN) is a method of exchange inside of the Munchee ecosystem, built to facilitate meaningful advertising and promotion for restaurant owners while building a quality base of content by rewarding content creators for their activity.

"Token Burn" plan: Munchee can potentially burn (take out of circulation) a small fraction of MUN tokens every time a restaurant pays us as advertising fee, which could potentially increase the appreciation of the remaining MUN tokens as the total supply in circulation reduces.

The MUN token (ERC20) will also be trade-able on cryptocurrency exchanges after the token sale.

​​Munchee users are encouraged to become active partners in maintaining the quality of the content posted in the platform by being rewarded with MUNs for their time and creativity. They can use their earned MUNs to pay for their meals/services at participating restaurants. Restaurant owners can attract more users by advertising through advertising and marketing options in Munchee. They will have an option to pay for the ad space using MUN tokens received from their customers.

FUNDING USAGE BREAKDOWN

There will be a total and finite supply of 500,000,000 MUN. Of that supply, 225,000,000 MUN will be listed for sale as part of the token pre-sale and sale events; with 275,000,000 MUN held in reserve by the company and introduced to the market in various ways, including bounties, team compensation, testing, and to manage rewards in the future.

The Munchee team supply tokens will be locked for 6 months after the end of the token sale.

  • 35% of funds collected will be applied to marketing, user acquisition, and promotion schedule for the existing Munchee mobile application.

  • 40% of funds collected will be applied to growing the development team.

  • 10% of funds collected will go towards legals to make sure Munchee is compliant in all countries.

  • 15% of funds collected will be reserved for maintenance and to ensure the smooth operation of the MUN token ecosystem.
